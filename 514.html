Glam Spam

Glam Spam is a great on the go mid day snack for the busy astronaut. Enjoy!

  • 1/2 lb wild boar rump
  • 1 c sparkles
  • 3 tbs rainbow glitter
  • 2 tsp gold flakes
  • 2 tbs brown sugar
  • 2 tsp himalayan pink salt

Cooking Instruction:

  1. Hunt down invasive wild boar and clean rump meat thoroughly. Overgrind into a fine paste.
  2. In mixing bowl whip in fresh sparkles.
  3. Slowly sprinkle in rainbow glitter sprinkles.
  4. Spread boar paste mixture into 8 x 4 buttered loaf pan.
  5. Coat top of mixture with gold flakes, brown sugar, and himalayan pink salt.
  6. Bake at 451° Farenheit for 47 minutes.
  7. Can loaf of meat and let age at least 6 weeks before eating.
